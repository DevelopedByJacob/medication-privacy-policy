Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Simple Pill Reminder (the “App”), developed by developedbyjacob.

Contact email: kiktruedeveloper@gmail.com

1. Overview

Simple Pill Reminder is designed to help users create medication reminders, mark medicines as taken or skipped, and view basic medication history.

The App is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, dosage recommendations, or emergency services. Always follow the instructions provided by your doctor, pharmacist, or qualified healthcare professional.

2. Information stored by the App

The App may allow you to enter information such as:

  • Medication name
  • Dose or dosage text
  • Reminder times
  • Medication schedule
  • Whether a medication was marked as taken, skipped, or pending
  • Medication history created by your use of the App

This information is stored locally on your device. The App does not require an account and does not upload your medication data to servers controlled by developedbyjacob.

3. Health-related data

Medication names, doses, schedules, and medication history may be considered health-related or sensitive information. The App is designed to keep this information on your device unless you choose to share or export it using features provided by the App or your device.

You are responsible for reviewing the information you enter in the App and for ensuring that your reminders match the instructions given by your healthcare professional.

4. Photo import and text recognition

The App may include an optional feature that allows you to select a photo of a medication package or label in order to extract text and help fill medication fields such as name or dose.

Text recognition is performed only to assist data entry. The detected text may be inaccurate or incomplete. You must always review and confirm the information before saving it.

The App does not use this feature to provide medical advice, identify treatments, recommend doses, or automatically decide how a medication should be taken.

The App does not intentionally store the selected photo after processing it, unless a future version clearly states otherwise and asks for the required permission or consent.

5. Advertising

The App may display advertisements using Google AdMob, a mobile advertising service provided by Google.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information from your device, such as advertising identifiers, approximate location, device information, app interactions, crash information, and other data as described in Google’s own policies.

developedbyjacob does not use medication names, doses, schedules, or medication history to personalize advertisements.

Depending on your region, you may be shown a consent form or privacy options related to advertising. For users in regions such as the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, consent may be requested where required by applicable law and Google policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

6. Freemium model and purchases

The App may be offered under a freemium model. Some features may be available for free, while optional premium features may require a one-time purchase or another purchase model offered through Google Play.

Payments and purchase processing are handled by Google Play. developedbyjacob does not receive or store your full payment card details.

7. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you about medication times. These notifications are generated based on the information you enter in the App.

You can manage or disable notifications through your device settings.

8. Data sharing

developedbyjacob does not sell your personal information or medication data.

The App does not share your medication information with family members, healthcare providers, advertisers, or other third parties automatically.

If the App includes a manual share or export feature, the information is shared only when you choose to do so using your device’s sharing options, such as email, messaging apps, or file export.

9. Data retention and deletion

Medication data and reminder history are stored locally on your device until you delete them within the App, clear the App’s data, or uninstall the App.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored data may be deleted according to your device and Android system behavior.

10. Children

The App is not directed to children. The App should be used by adults or under the supervision of a parent, guardian, caregiver, or qualified healthcare professional where appropriate.

11. Security

The App is designed to store medication data locally on your device. You are responsible for keeping your device secure, including using screen lock, passwords, biometric protection, or other security features available on your device.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and developedbyjacob cannot guarantee absolute security.
